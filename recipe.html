Biyani

Chicken biryani is a celebrated South Asian dish featuring tender, yogurt-marinated chicken slow-cooked with aromatic basmati rice, warm spices, and herbs


Ingredients

  • rice
  • chicken
  • biryani masala
  • tomatos
  • onion
  • yogurt

Instructions

  1. first fry onion to brown.
  2. marinate chicken with adding fried onion,biryani masala and yogurt.
  3. after marination , put it into oil to fry and adding tomatos.

It will take 20 minute to cook and will server in 30 minutes.
